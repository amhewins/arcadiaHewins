Laundry Infographic for Non-English Speaking Students

Philadelphia University hosts many students from outside the country who may not know how our laundry machines work. This infographic is meant to show a person who speaks any language how to do laundry in the machines provided.

Get Active Infographic

There are so many opportunities to get outside and get active within Philadelphia. It is easy to simply assume because it is a major city that that is not the case, so the job of the banner was to prove that. Collaboration with a geospatial analyst brought this banner to life with a map that easily shows all active parts of Philadelphia. Calm tones were used to invite any personailty to view and use the content shown. The green was used to link a brain to grass and greenery outside beacuse the banner was designed with a display indoor in mind.
